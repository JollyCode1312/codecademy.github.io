Headings and Text Styles

Difference between paragraphs and headings

H1: The main heading for pages

  1. The biggest Heading
  2. Suitable top lines
  3. Not usable for long texts

H2: subheadings

  1. Like in newspaper, H1 then H2
  2. Style can differ from H1
  3. Font, size, family

p: paragraph text

  1. p is best for longer texts or paragraphs
  2. article can be a synonym
  3. lol
